Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to register to use Gatepass?
    Yes.
  • Can I buy tickets on the Gatepass website?
    No. Tickets can only be bought through our mobile platforms.
  • How long will the ticket bought on Gatepass be valid?
    Until the day or time after the event.
  • How much will I be charged to use Gatepass as an event organiser?
    10% of total ticket sales.
  • How long will it take to cashout my ticket sales on Gatepass as an organiser?
    24 hours after the event has taken place.
  • How much will I be charged to use Gatepass as a user or ticket buyer?
    0%. Using Gatepass is absolutely free.
  • How many tickets can I buy with Gatepass?
    As many as are available.
  • I checked myself in by accident. Can I undo or uncheck it?
    No. Gatepass is not responsible for tickets that are checked-in by unauthorized personnel.
  • If I logout, will I still have my online tickets?
    Yes.
  • If I logout, will I still have my offline tickets?
    No. All offline tickets are cleared when you logout.
  • Will I be charged extra during ticket purchase?
    No. We charge only the exact price of the ticket.
  • Can I get a refund on tickets bought using Gatepass?
    No. Tickets are non-refundable but transferable.
  • Will I be charged for transferring tickets?
    No. Transferring or sharing tickets on Gatepass is absolutely free.
